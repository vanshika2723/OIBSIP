Marie Curie

Pioneer Scientist in Radioactivity

About Marie Curie

Marie Curie was a physicist and chemist who conducted pioneering research on radioactivity. She was the first woman to win a Nobel Prize and the only person to win Nobel Prizes in two different sciences. Her discoveries changed the world of science forever.

Major Achievements

  • First woman to win a Nobel Prize
  • Discovered Polonium and Radium
  • Founded Curie Institutes in Paris and Warsaw
  • Contributed to medical research with X-ray use in WWI

Inspiration

"Nothing in life is to be feared, it is only to be understood." – Marie Curie
